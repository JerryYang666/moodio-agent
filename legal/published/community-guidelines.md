# MOODIO COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

**Last Updated:** March 24, 2026
**Effective Date:** March 24, 2026

---

## Welcome to Moodio

Moodio is a creative platform where filmmakers, visual artists, and content creators discover cinematic references and generate new visual content using AI. These Community Guidelines exist to keep Moodio a safe, respectful, and inspiring space for everyone. They supplement our Terms of Service and Acceptable Use Policy.

---

## Our Principles

**Create responsibly.** AI generation is a powerful creative tool. Use it thoughtfully and consider the impact of what you create.

**Respect others.** Respect the rights, privacy, and dignity of every person — whether they are another Moodio user, a public figure, or someone depicted in your content.

**Be honest.** Be transparent about what is AI-generated, especially when your content depicts real people or realistic events.

**Respect intellectual property.** The Search Library is for reference and inspiration. The original creators own their work. Your AI-generated outputs should reflect your own creative vision, not replicate others' protected expression.

---

## Content Standards

### What's Encouraged
- Using the Search Library for creative reference, mood boards, and visual research
- Generating original creative content for personal projects, commercial work, and artistic expression
- Sharing your work with the community (if you choose to make it public)
- Providing constructive feedback to other creators
- Experimenting with AI tools to push creative boundaries

### What's Not Allowed

**Harmful content.** Do not create content depicting violence toward specific individuals, content that promotes self-harm or suicide, or content designed to harass or intimidate. See our Acceptable Use Policy for the complete list of prohibited content.

**Non-consensual content.** Do not generate sexual, intimate, or degrading content depicting real identifiable individuals without their explicit consent. This includes "deepfakes" and any content that places a real person in a fabricated scenario without disclosure.

**Deceptive content.** Do not create realistic-looking content that could be mistaken for authentic documentation of real events — particularly involving real public figures, political events, or newsworthy situations — without clearly labeling it as AI-generated. Satire and parody are acceptable when a reasonable viewer would understand the content is not authentic.

**Child safety.** Any content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and law enforcement.

**Hate and discrimination.** Do not create content that promotes hatred or violence against individuals or groups based on protected characteristics. This includes content that dehumanizes, promotes stereotypes for the purpose of harm, or calls for exclusion or violence.

**Spam and manipulation.** Do not use the platform to distribute spam, conduct phishing, spread malware, or manipulate engagement metrics.

---

## AI Disclosure Requirements

When you share AI-generated content outside of Moodio (on social media, in publications, in presentations, etc.), we strongly encourage you to disclose that the content was created with AI assistance. In the following situations, disclosure is **required**:

- When the content depicts or closely resembles a real, identifiable person
- When the content could reasonably be interpreted as documentation of a real event
- When required by the laws of the jurisdiction where you distribute the content (e.g., EU AI Act, China AI Content Labeling Rules)
- When required by the platform where you distribute the content

A simple disclosure such as "Created with AI" or "AI-generated using Moodio" is sufficient.

## Search Library Etiquette

The Search Library contains visual content from commercially released films, TV shows, commercials, and other productions. This content belongs to its original copyright holders. When using the Search Library:

- Use content for reference, inspiration, mood boards, and creative planning
- Do not download, redistribute, or present Search Library content as your own
- Do not use Search Library content in final productions or commercial outputs
- If sharing mood boards or reference boards externally, credit the original source where known

## Enforcement

We use a combination of automated detection systems and human review to enforce these Guidelines. Enforcement is graduated:

**First violation (minor):** Content removal + educational notice explaining the violation.

**Second violation or first serious violation:** Content removal + formal warning. Upload/generation privileges may be temporarily restricted.

**Third violation or severe violation:** Temporary account suspension or permanent termination, depending on severity. Remaining credits are forfeited.

**Immediate termination (zero tolerance):** CSAM, credible threats of violence, non-consensual intimate imagery, or systematic/commercial-scale infringement result in immediate permanent termination without prior warning.

---

## Appeals

If you believe enforcement action was taken in error, you may appeal within 30 days by emailing support@moodio.art. Include your username, the content or action in question, and your explanation. We will review appeals promptly and notify you of the outcome.

---

## Reporting

If you encounter content that violates these Guidelines, please report it:

- **In-App:** Use the "Report" button on any piece of content
- **Email:** support@moodio.art
- **Urgent safety concerns:** support@moodio.art

All reports are reviewed. You will not face retaliation for reporting in good faith.

---

## Changes

We may update these Community Guidelines as our platform evolves and as laws and norms around AI-generated content develop. We will notify users of material changes through the Service.
