# MOODIO REFUND AND CANCELLATION POLICY

**Last Updated:** March 24, 2026
**Effective Date:** March 24, 2026

---

## 1. OVERVIEW

This Refund and Cancellation Policy explains how refunds and cancellations work for Moodio subscriptions and credit purchases. This policy is part of and incorporated into the Moodio Terms of Service.

---

## 2. CANCELLATION

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by emailing support@moodio.art. When you cancel, your paid features and remaining credits stay active until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged for subsequent periods.

---

## 3. REFUNDS

**3.1 General Policy.** All purchases (subscriptions and credit packs) are generally non-refundable. However, we may issue refunds at our discretion in the following circumstances:

- **Duplicate charges:** If you were charged multiple times for the same purchase due to a technical error.
- **Failed generations:** Credits consumed by generation requests that fail due to Moodio system errors are automatically refunded to your credit balance (not as cash).
- **Unauthorized charges:** If you believe you were charged without authorization, contact us promptly.

**3.2 Requesting a Refund.** To request a refund, email support@moodio.art with your account email and a description of the issue. We will review each request on a case-by-case basis.

**3.3 EU/EEA Users.** If you are located in the EU/EEA, you may have a right of withdrawal under applicable consumer protection laws. By using the Service and initiating a generation or accessing paid content, you consent to immediate performance and acknowledge that your right of withdrawal is lost once the service has been fully performed.

---

## 4. NO REFUND ON TERMINATION FOR VIOLATIONS

If Moodio terminates your account for violation of our Terms of Service, Acceptable Use Policy, or Community Guidelines, you are not entitled to a refund of any subscription fees or unused credits.

---

## 5. CHARGEBACKS

If you initiate a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or payment provider, we reserve the right to suspend your account pending resolution. We encourage you to contact support@moodio.art first — we can typically resolve issues faster than the chargeback process.

---

## 6. REFUND METHOD

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method. Processing times depend on your payment provider.

---

## 7. CONTACT

For refund and billing inquiries: support@moodio.art
