## 2. CREDIT SYSTEM

**2.1 How Credits Work.** Moodio uses a credit-based system for AI generation features. Each generation action consumes a specified number of credits. The credit cost for each action is displayed before you confirm the generation.

**2.2 Purchasing Credits.** Credits may be purchased as one-time credit packs or as part of a subscription plan. Prices are displayed on the Pricing Page. Prices are subject to change; however, price changes will not affect credits already purchased or active subscription periods already paid for.

**2.3 Credit Consumption.** Credits are consumed at the time a generation request is submitted. If a generation fails due to a Moodio system error (not a content policy violation), the credits will be automatically refunded to your account. Credits are not refunded for generations that complete successfully but produce results you are dissatisfied with.

**2.4 Credit Expiration.** Credits may expire as described on the Pricing Page. If no expiration is specified, purchased credits do not expire while your account remains active.

**2.5 No Monetary Value.** Credits have no cash value and cannot be exchanged for cash, transferred to other users, or redeemed outside the Service.
