## 4. ROYALTIES AND PAYMENT

**4.1 Royalty Rate.** In consideration for the license granted herein, Licensee shall pay Licensor a royalty equal to 8% of Net Revenue (the "Royalty"), payable quarterly.

**4.2 Minimum Royalty.** Beginning in the second year of this Agreement, Licensee shall pay a minimum annual royalty of USD 10,000 (the "Minimum Royalty"), regardless of actual Net Revenue. Quarterly royalty payments shall be credited against the Minimum Royalty.

**4.3 Payment Terms.** Royalty payments are due within thirty (30) days after the end of each calendar quarter. Payments shall be made in U.S. dollars via wire transfer to an account designated by Licensor. Licensee is responsible for obtaining all necessary foreign exchange approvals.

**4.4 Withholding Tax.** All royalty payments are subject to applicable PRC withholding tax. Licensee shall withhold and remit such taxes to the appropriate PRC tax authorities and provide Licensor with official tax payment certificates within thirty (30) days of each payment. Under the current US-China tax treaty, the withholding tax rate on royalties is 10%.

**4.5 VAT.** Licensee shall be responsible for any PRC value-added tax (currently 6% for technology licensing) and applicable surcharges imposed on royalty payments. Licensee shall provide Licensor with valid VAT invoices as required.

**4.6 Records and Audit.** Licensee shall maintain accurate books and records of all revenue related to the China Platform. Licensor shall have the right, upon thirty (30) days' written notice, to audit Licensee's books and records no more than once per year, at Licensor's expense (unless a discrepancy of more than 5% is found, in which case Licensee bears the cost).

**4.7 MOFCOM Registration.** Licensee shall register this Agreement with the relevant provincial commerce authority (MOFCOM/DOFCOM) within sixty (60) days of the Effective Date, as required under PRC technology import regulations. Licensor shall cooperate with such registration.
